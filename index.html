SIG MKII

(Story Idea Generator Mark 2)

Protagonist
Action
Word

Hello, Esteemed Internet Wanderer, and welcome to this app!

It pulls random words from a few arrays and displays the words on the DOM. The story you write in response to the words is open to interpretation. The nifty thing is that it does this on a 3D cube that spins.

If you'd like to know some more about it, not that's there's much more to know, you can check out the repo here: REPO
